A short tour of world scripts

Text on the modern web is Unicode, and an extractor that mangles non-ASCII characters quietly corrupts meaning. A café menu in Paris, a greeting in 日本語, and a proverb in Ελληνικά must all survive the round trip intact.

Emoji are text too: a 🌍 in the source should still be a 🌍 in the extracted markdown, not a question mark or a broken escape. Accented names such as Gödel, Škoda and Œuvre are the everyday case that matters most.
